    Overwrite SQL Configurations

    Your can overwrite ClickHouse SQL Configurations by editing $SPARK_HOME/conf/spark-defaults.conf, e.g.

    spark.clickhouse.write.batchSize          10000
spark.clickhouse.write.maxRetry           2
